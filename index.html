What is Core?

- Core is a private investment group founded in 2017 and is currently operational in 3 continents - Europe, North America & Africa

What sectors does Core focus on?

- We focus on 6 sectors- Healthcare, Hospitality, Finance, Media, Technology and Transportation. We buyout and invest capital in business and solutions within these key sectors and provide value globally.

Where is Core registered?

- We are registered as an investment group in the United Kingdom and Wales and in the State of Georgia (USA) and in Nigeria.
